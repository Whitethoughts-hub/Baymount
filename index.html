Project Highlights

Timeless Heritage

The community features an enshrined temple, and around it, 50-year-old tamarind and banyan trees, giving the place its grounding.

Scenic Vistas

The topography moves in free-form, rising and dipping with its natural contours.

Enduring Build

Each villa starts with a strong concrete foundation and continues with fittings chosen for lasting reliability.

Green Streets

Every street is edged with pocket gardens that soften the built environment and bring nature into daily life.

Central Meadow

Set beside an expansive central park, the villas enjoy a natural extension of open space right at their doorstep.

Seamless Car Parking

The layout ensures ample parking that keeps vehicles out of sight and the streetscape free of visual clutter.
